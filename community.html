Deltahacks

Deltahacks is a hackathon with a theme of creating positive social impact. Each year, 500 attendees come to McMaster University and build software projects from scratch in a single weekend. The event aims to both empower expert coders to create projects for positive change as well as encourage beginners to start programming in a nurturing environments.

    Workshop and Events Coordinater (2017-Present)

    I collaborate with the Deltahacks logistics team to plan and coordinate events and workshops for McMaster's fourth annual hackathon, as well as generate and implement ideas to improve the hackathon and create a better attendee experience. I help coordinate busses from other universities, manage the budget, and contact vendors for pricing.

    Social Media (2016-2017)

    I helped manage the hackathon's Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. I advertised the event on various platforms. During the event, I helped manage the Slack, Facebook and Twitter to keep attendees up-to-date on events during the hackathon. I also took photographs for the event's Instagram and Snapchat.
